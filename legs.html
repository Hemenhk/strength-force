Legs

Deadlift

5 sets of 5 reps

1. Stand in the middle of the bar, with the bar touching your shins.
2. Bend down and grip the bar a bit wider than shoulder width.
3. Bring your backside down and stick your chest up, whilst also tucking your chin.
4. With a straight back, pull the bar upwards until you are standing upright.
5. Slowly bring the bar back down in the same manner in which you brought it up. Repeat.

Lats Pulldown

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Balance both arms, and bring your elbows slightly below 180 degrees, so that they are at an angle.
2. Press the dumbells upwards and bring them slightly together at the top for a better contraction.
3. Bring the elbows down to a 90 degree angle. Further and you risk hurting your front delts.

Seated Cable Row

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Whilst seated, grab the handle and lean back, all whilst keeping your back straight.
2. Pull the handle towards your stomach and squeeze for a better contraction.
3. Slowly bring the handles back and stop juist before the weights are restacked, then repeat.

One Arm Dumbbell Row

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Set the cables to be below your knees.
2. Grab the handles and bring your hands to meet right below your chin. Squeeze for a better contraction.
3. Slowly bring the handles back down, but stop right before they come to a full stop, so as to keep the muscles in action.

Biceps

Barbell Curl

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Grab the barbell shoulder width, and stand upright.
2. Curl the barbell, with locked elbows, towards your chin.
3. Slowly bring the barbell down, and stop before your arms extend fully. Repeat.

Dumbell Curls

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Grab the dumbbells whilst seated on a bench with a sight decline.
2. Curl the dumbbells and squeeze for a better contraction.
3. Slowly bring the dumbbells down and stop before your arms extend fully. Repeat.

Hammer Curls

3 sets of 10 reps

1. Grab the dumbbells in either hand.
2. Face the palms of your hands inwards towards you.
3. Curl the dumbbells in a controlled motion.
4. Slowly bring the dumbbells down, and stop before you extend your arms fully.
